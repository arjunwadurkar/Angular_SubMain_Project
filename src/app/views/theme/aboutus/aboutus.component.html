About Us

Established in the year 2000, we have led the way in designing, developing, and maintaining diverse IT Solutions tailored for product management and e-commerce businesses of all sizes. Today, in addition to crafting custom web and mobile applications for product management and e-commerce, Ecotech guides clients worldwide in creating customized software systems based on SAP ERP, RPA, AI, and IoT technologies to streamline operations, enhance customer experiences, and drive growth in the digital marketplace.

Since the beginning, we have worked at the intersection of IT and core industries. This has given us the prerequisite expertise and empathy that is critical to develop unique business solutions for unique business situations and customer expectations.
